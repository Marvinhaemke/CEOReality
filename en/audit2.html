Open Questions

The honest
answers.

Is the audit really free?
Yes. There's no credit card entry, no trial, no hidden renewal. You fill out the form, we analyze, you get a 6-page PDF report. What you do with it afterwards is entirely your decision.
How does the audit differ from a quiz?
A quiz compares your answers against a point scale. An audit is a written diagnosis. We check your website, your funnel, your positioning, your numerical statements and write an individual report. Four hours of work per audit, on average.
How long until I receive the report?
Maximum 72 hours from receipt of the request. In most cases faster. We analyze on business days. If you submit on Friday evening, the report will be with you by Wednesday at the latest.
Will I be pitched after the report?
There's no automatic sales call. We'll reach out briefly after the report to ask if you have questions or want to talk. If not, the matter is settled. We don't follow up persistently.
What does the CEO Reality program cost afterwards?
CEO Reality is a premium 1-on-1 mentoring program across four phases and 120 days. We discuss the exact price transparently in the audit follow-up call, if it comes to that. You'll get the report regardless.
What happens to my data?
Your information is used exclusively to create your audit report and for subsequent contact. It is not shared, not sold and not used for anonymized statistics or training data. Details can be found in our Privacy Policy.
What if you reject my audit?
We decline when your profile clearly doesn't fit our ICP — for example when you don't have revenue yet or we're not the right partner for your industry. In that case you'll get a brief, honest explanation instead of a standard analysis that doesn't help you.
